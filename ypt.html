The Troop 1441 Youth Protection Policy:
Every Adult associated with Troop 1441 (not only those registered with the Boy Scouts of America) must have completed two Youth Protection Programs to participate in Troop 1441 activities:
1) BSA's Youth Protection Training (YPT, renewed every two years), and
2) The Catholic Church's one-time-only Virtus Training and background check

Activities where compliance is suggested, but not required:
- Non-participatory attendance at troop meetings, Courts of Honor, Social events (Picnics. etc).
- Assistance as labor on Eagle Projects.

This policy is implemented for the protection of adults and youths.

Complete the following steps for compliance:

1) BSA Youth Protection Training: YPT
Please save an electronic version (pdf) of the certificate, and forward it to Mr Allen.

2) Virtus Training and Background Check:
Register online at VIRTUS.ORG
After your 3 hour in-person training, initiate the electronic background check at the SJB Parish Office Contact 301-622-1122 or sjbbulletin@sjbcatholicchurch.com to schedule the brief appointment. Please do not show up at the office without an appointment. Two forms of ID including one Photo ID (i.e. Driver's License) and any one of the following: Birth Certificate, Social Security Card, Passport, Naturalization Card Original Adult Volunteer Application Form Acknowledgement Page from the Child Protection Policy Booklet
The background check takes about 3 weeks; SJB is notified only if there is a problem. Your compliance with the youth protection policies will be considered complete about three weeks after the background check is initiated.
