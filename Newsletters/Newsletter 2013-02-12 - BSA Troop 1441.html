Newsletter 2013-02-12

posted Feb 19, 2013, 8:10 AM by Troop Webmaster   [ updated Mar 11, 2013, 7:37 AM ]
The troop survived its 33rd BroadCreek Father and Son camp-in the weekend of 2/1-2/3.  We had two cabins, both filled to capacity, 37 attendees in all.  The scouts spent the day Saturday tackling a number of outdoor challenges as patrols.  They were scored on participation, success, teamwork and enthusiasm. the events included matchless firebuilding, compass work, shelter building and a teamwork challenge.
both patrols scored well with only two points separating them at the end.  Each year we hope for snow and we got about an inch Saturday night.
 
Order of the Arrow Elections
The troop will hold its annual OA election this Thursday at the troop meeting.  The Order of the Arrow is the Honor Camping organization within the BSA and to qualify a scout must be First Class, have 15 nights of Camping, including one long term camp (minimum 6 nights), have Scoutmaster approval and receive at least 51 % of the vote.
 
Troop Senior Patrol Leader and Patrol Leader Elections
The troop will also hold its semi-annual leadership elections the same evening as the OA elections. Everyone is needed at the meeting to make the election a success. 
 
There will be NO troop meeting on February 21st due to Parish Activities week at St. John's.  The regular meeting schedule will resume the following week.
 
The Ski Trip is a go for the weekend of March 1-2-3.
 
Service Project
The troop will be particpating in the Stop Hunger Now event being held at Colesville Presbyterian Church on Saturday March 9th from 8:30 AM to 1 PM.  We will be packing food bags as we have done before. it is a Class A uniform event and service hours can be earned.
 
Philmont High Adventure Preparations.
Mr Ingeholm will be conducting a number of training events over the next few months to get the crews ready to tackle the mountains in New Mexico this summer.
