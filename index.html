TROOP 1441

Troop 1441 is a troop of boys, and it is part of the White Oak District of the National Capital Area Council of Scouts BSA. The troop was founded in June of 1968. Meetings are typically 7:00pm to 8:45pm Monday evenings at Saint John the Baptist Catholic Church in Silver Spring. Please contact John Wnek prior to a visit to assure that the meeting will be held.
